- GOOD: "I've spent my career producing integrated campaigns across film, TV, digital, social, interactive, live events, and audience focused experiences. More recently, [company] services have become part of my day to day production workflow."

- BAD: "For the past three years at RF Studio53, I served as the primary client contact..."
- GOOD: "At RF Studio53, I managed Fortune 500 accounts across healthcare, pharma, finance..."
- The fix: Name the role/company directly without the temporal preamble.

**Pattern 3: Fanboying the company**
Stating admiration or enthusiasm about the company instead of operational connection. Flag and rewrite.
- BAD: "I've been an admirer of [company]'s mission since..." / "I'm passionate about what your team is building..."
- GOOD: "[Company] services have become part of my day to day production workflow. I use them to [specific operational uses]."
- The fix: State the connection as what you DO with the company's product/work, not how you FEEL about it.
